About Me

I'm Hiram Jiménez, a mechanical engineering student from Tecnológico de Monterrey in Mexico with a strong passion for design, simulation and innovation. Throughout my academic journey and professional experiences I've had the opportunity to get hands-on experience in computer-aided design (CAD), fluid dynamics and mechanical and electrical products design.

My interests have always been tied to the automotive industry, where I aim and look for opportunities to contribute in the areas of powertrains and aerodynamics. Aside my technical experience and eagerness to learn, I bring a creative mindset and great attitude towards engineering challenges that involve thinking outside the box.

This porfolio showcases some of the projects I've been part of that I consider best reflect my technical knowledge and interests, besides showing the evolution of my personal journey as an engineer in constant growth.
